Creating Ethnomusicpreneurs

About Us

Salute to our ethnomusic heroes who are preserving our culture and tradition because we exist only when culture and tradition exist. Baja Nepal is creating 10,000 ethnomusicpreneurs through a digital platform to connect our ethnomusicians from rural Nepal to national and international markets. It is a two sided marketplace driven by social cause to fight caste discrimination, physical disabilities, gender inequality, poverty, unemployment and ethnomusic extinction where you can find our ethnomusicians from every nook and corner of 77 districts of Nepal. This is a solution for any problem related to ethnomusic. The more we enjoy our ethnomusic, the more we contribute to its preservation.

Baja Nepal is an idea about giving oppurtinity to underprivilegde communities from mechi to makahali. It is the common digital platform to connect the traditional bajauney( musicians) to different events like wedding , ceremonies , welcome programms , bratabandha etc.

Why Us?

For Ethnomusicians:
- If you are a professional or freelancing ethnomusician or musical instrument maker, this is the platform which will help you to generate income. First you or your representative need to register you or your group under our platform. Do not worry! It is free. If you feel bored or confused about registering, we are there to register you with your one gesture.
For customers:
- If you are in the search of ethnomusicians or ethnomusic or ethnomusical instrument with quality and affordable price, this is the perfect solution for you. Here you can hire our musicians for any event or collaborate with them for music works and also purchase their music or musical instruments. If you need more support from us, we are always there for you to support. Once you tell your problem, we take whole responsibility to keep you completely satisfied. Sometimes we may not know where we need musicians? So here are our few tips. For collaboration in music composition For live events For virtual events For birthday, wedding, bratabandha etc. Music training (in person or virtually) Music therapies Special surprise to your favourite ones Customized music (even if you are abroad, our musician will sing for you virtually) Musical Instruments making Farewell programmes, welcome programmes, festivals Still if our tips could not help, give us a chance to help you to figure out the exact need.
